Diamond Cut Coconut

Our signature carved opening — an elegant faceted top that's easy to sip from and beautiful to serve. Each coconut is hand-cut by skilled harvesters and chilled to lock in its natural sweetness, making it the perfect centrepiece for cafés, events, and health-conscious sipping.

Easy Open Coconut

A pull-tab style top that pops open in seconds — fresh hydration without a knife or any fuss. Ideal for on-the-go customers and retail shelves, the easy-open design keeps the water sealed and sterile until the very moment you're ready to drink.

03
Coconut with Straw

Coconut with Straw

Pre-fitted with an eco-friendly straw — grab, sip, and go for instant hydration on the move. A favourite for beaches, gyms, and festivals, this ready-to-drink format pairs convenience with zero plastic waste thanks to its biodegradable straw.

Sprout Coconut

Sprout Coconut

The spongy, naturally sweet coconut sprout — a rare delicacy prized for its soft, candy-like texture. Formed inside germinating coconuts, the sprout is rich in fibre and a beloved healthy snack across the tropics, eaten fresh or added to desserts.

05
Polish Coconut

Polish Coconut

A cleanly de-husked, machine-polished mature coconut with a smooth shell and long shelf life. Ready for retail and export, polished coconuts protect the rich white meat inside — perfect for milk, oil, and culinary use worldwide.

06
Coconut Meat

Coconut Meat

Thick, fresh white coconut flesh — scooped from mature coconuts and ready for kitchens of every kind. Packed with healthy fats and fibre, the meat is used for coconut milk, oil, flour, and shredded toppings, delivering rich flavour and nourishment.
